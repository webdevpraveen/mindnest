MindNest — Student Mental Wellness Community
A peer-support platform designed specifically around how academic stress actually affects Indian college students — with gamified wellness and real rewards.
College Project
Problem statement addressed
Problem 1
Academic pressure
Students face exam anxiety, CGPA pressure, family expectations, and attendance rules with no structured peer support. They suffer in isolation, not knowing others feel the same way.
Problem 2
Social comparison
WhatsApp group toppers, LinkedIn internship posts, and Instagram highlight reels make students compare their worst days to others' best — silently destroying their self-worth.
6 features — how each one solves the problem
🏡
Community Feed
Students post about academic struggles, wins, or tips. Anonymous toggle means they can share without identity fear. Tags like #academics, #burnout, #cgpa-pressure create topic clusters. "I feel this" button validates emotions without forcing advice.
✅ Solves isolation — students see others going through the same thing
+10 pts per post
🕊️
Vent Room — anonymous safe space
Dedicated dark-themed room for heavy emotional venting. All posts are anonymous by default. Severity color coding (green/amber/red border) signals how serious a vent is. "Send Comfort DM" lets others privately reach out. "Flag for Counselor" escalates crisis posts.
✅ Gives students a pressure valve — without identity exposure
+10 pts per validate
🌿
Breathe & Reset — guided breathing
Three clinically proven techniques: 4-7-8 (sleep/anxiety), Box Breathing (focus), Quick Calm (panic). Animated expanding circle with live countdown. Earns points on session completion to incentivize actual usage.
✅ Gives students an immediate, in-browser tool during panic/exam moments
+20 pts per session
📓
Private Journal
Daily private journaling with mood selector, word count, and past entry history. Completely private — not shared anywhere. Rewarded to encourage journaling as a mental health habit.
✅ Externalizing thoughts reduces rumination — clinically backed
+15 pts per entry
✉️
Messages — peer-to-peer chat
Direct + group messaging. "Comfort DM" system lets Vent Room readers reach out privately to anonymous venters. Study groups can coordinate here. Warm auto-replies simulate active conversation for demo.
✅ Builds real human connection beyond just posts — combats loneliness
+15 pts per comfort DM
🏆
Wellness Rewards — points to real vouchers
Every wellness action (check-in, journal, breathe, validate) earns points. Points redeem into real vouchers: CCD, Amazon Books, Spotify, Swiggy, Udemy courses. 7-day streak bonus incentivizes consistency.
✅ Gamification converts passive empathy into active daily wellness habits
500–2000 pts to redeem
Exclusive innovations — not found on any existing platform
📊
Pressure Points Heatmap
Community mood check-ins are aggregated in real-time into a subject-wise stress bar chart — "Exams 85%, Family 55%, Finances 48%". Students see what's stressing the most students this week. No other platform does this.
🫂
"I feel this" Validate — not advice
Most platforms have Like/Comment. MindNest replaces this with "I feel this" — which signals emotional solidarity, not judgment or advice-giving. Designed around how students actually want to be heard.
🔴
Vent Severity System
Left-border color on vent cards (green/amber/red) based on self-reported stress level. Community can quickly scan for who needs urgent support, and the "Flag for Counselor" button exists only here for serious cases.
